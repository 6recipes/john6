Pinoy Style Pork Adobo

Description

Ingredients Ingredients: 1/2 pork belly, cut into cubes 1/4 cup soy sauce 1/4 cup vinegar 1/4 cup water 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 medium onion, chopped 2 bayleaves 1 ground black pepper 1 pinch umami 2 hard boiled egg

Cooking

1

Prepare the ingredients needed while boiling the eggs.

2

Saute the onion and garlic. Add pork until it turns brown and simmer for 10mins. Cover. Add black pepper, bayleaves and umami and wait for another 5 mins.

3

Add the hard boiled egg. Serve and enjoy eating! ðŸ˜‰
